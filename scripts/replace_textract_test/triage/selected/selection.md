## Diversity Coverage

- **Page counts**: 1p (x7), 2p (x3), 3p (x2), 5p, 8p, 9p, 12p, 13p
- **10+ pages for chunk boundary testing**: Ferreira Fresh (13p), RTMS (12p)
- **Handwriting/annotations**: Darling Romery (011b) - red marks
- **Poor scan quality**: Ladismith Cheese (084b)
- **Non-English/bilingual**: Impact Meter (EN/AF), Cape Fire (AF header), Darling Romery (AF/EN)
- **Date formats**: DD/MM/YYYY, YYYY/MM/DD, DD-Mon-YY, DD-MM-YY, DD Month YYYY
- **Number formats**: 1,234.56 (comma thousands) and 1 234.56 (space thousands)
- **Layout types**: simple table, multi-section, complex nested, non-standard letter, running ledger, monospaced report, wide multi-column
- **Visual density**: sparse through dense
