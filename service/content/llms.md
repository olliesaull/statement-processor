# Statement Processor

> Supplier statement reconciliation for Xero. Upload a PDF statement, review extracted line items, match them against your Xero invoices and payments, and export the results.

## Who it's for

Statement Processor is built for accountants, bookkeepers, and businesses that use Xero. It eliminates manual checking of supplier statements against accounting records.

## How it works

1. Connect your Xero organisation
2. Upload a supplier statement PDF
3. Line items are automatically extracted from the PDF
4. Review and match each line item against Xero invoices and payments
5. Export the reconciled results to Excel

## Key features

- Automatic line item extraction from PDF supplier statements
- Matching against Xero invoices, credit notes, and payments
- Side-by-side reconciliation view with match suggestions
- Excel export of reconciliation results
- Multi-tenant support (manage multiple Xero organisations)
- Pay-as-you-go token billing (no subscription)
