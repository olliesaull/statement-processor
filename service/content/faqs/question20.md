Yes. You can disconnect your Xero account at any time from the Tenant Management page using the "Disconnect" button. When you disconnect, you will be asked how you would like us to handle your data:

- **Delete within 24 hours** — your statements and cached Xero data are permanently removed within 24 hours.
- **Delete after 2 weeks** — a grace period in case you change your mind. Reconnect within 2 weeks and your data is still there.
- **Delete after 1 year** — keeps your data available so you can return without reloading everything from Xero.

If you reconnect before your chosen deletion date, the pending deletion is automatically cancelled and your data is preserved.
