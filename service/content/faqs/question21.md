They serve different purposes:

- **Logout** ends your current browser session on Statement Processor. Your Xero connection and all your data remain intact. The next time you visit, you simply log back in and pick up where you left off.
- **Disconnect** removes the link between Statement Processor and your Xero organisation. You will be asked whether to keep or delete your stored data. If you later reconnect the same Xero tenant, Statement Processor will re-establish the connection and reload your data from Xero if needed.

In short: logout is temporary, disconnect is permanent (until you reconnect).
