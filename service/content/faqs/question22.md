When you disconnect, you choose one of three timelines: within 24 hours, after 2 weeks, or after 1 year. A background process runs daily and permanently removes your statement PDFs, extracted data, and cached Xero data (contacts, invoices, payments, and credit notes) once your chosen period has passed.

Your billing history (token balance, purchase records) is retained for our records and is not deleted, as it does not contain sensitive financial data.

If you reconnect before the deletion date, the pending deletion is automatically cancelled.
