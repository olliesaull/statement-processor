About

Built to make supplier statement reconciliation simpler.

Statement Processor helps finance teams reduce manual checks, catch issues earlier, and keep a clear record of completed reconciliation work.

Why this exists

Reconciliation is often spread across PDFs, spreadsheets, and multiple accounting screens. Statement Processor brings those steps into one consistent workflow so teams can work faster and with fewer missed details.

Who It Is For

Designed for day-to-day finance operations

Finance Teams

Keep month-end statement review work organised and easier to track across the team.

Bookkeepers

Review supplier statements against Xero records in one place without constant context switching.

Non-Technical Users

Follow a clear step-by-step process without needing to understand technical setup details.

Use Cases

Where teams use it most

Monthly Reconciliation

Process supplier statements as they arrive and keep reviews moving consistently.

Exception Cleanup

Find mismatches and anomalies faster so effort is focused where it matters most.

Audit Preparation

Use completion tracking and exports to provide a clear reconciliation trail.

The Workflow

How it helps in practice

01

Upload supplier statement PDFs. Each file is matched to the correct supplier automatically.

02

Compare statement rows against Xero invoices, credit notes, and payments.

03

Review mismatches and anomalies, then mark rows and statements complete.

04

Download an Excel export when you need a shareable record of the review.

Results

Outcomes teams care about

  • Less manual line-by-line checking.
  • Clear visibility of what is complete and what still needs review.
  • More consistent reconciliation across people and periods.
